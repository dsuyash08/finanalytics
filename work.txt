For example, using the data of 2021 annual report, an analyst might have to forecast the earnings for next three years 2022, 2023, and 2024. The purpose of this question is to assess whether LLMs are capable of reasoning and prediction as well as a financial analyst can. Please note that you will be able to find actual values for years up to 2024. For 2025, you might not be able to find the actual value as results for 2025 will be announced in 2026. Please note the percentage difference between the predicted and actual values by using the following formula: Percentage Difference = ((Actual Value – Predicted Value)/Actual Value)*100.
2.	Comment on the accuracy of results along the two dimensions: a) Large-cap vs mid-cap vs small-cap companies, and b) accuracy as the forecasting horizon increases (for example, for 2021 annual report, the forecasting horizon is Year 1 (2022), Year 2 (2023), and Year 3 (2024)).
